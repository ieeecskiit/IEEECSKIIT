Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply to become a Member?

Any KIIT student passionate about technology and community building can apply. While prior experience is beneficial, it's not mandatory. We value enthusiasm, commitment, and a willingness to learn.

What is the time commitment required?

The time commitment varies based on your role and our event calendar. Typically, you need to contribute 5-10 hours per month, with slightly more during major events. We understand academic priorities and will work around your schedule.

What skills do I need to join?

Different roles require different skills. While technical roles may require specific knowledge, many positions only need enthusiasm, good communication skills, and a willingness to learn. We provide training for all members.

Will I receive any training?

Yes! All volunteers receive orientation and role-specific training. We also organize skill development sessions throughout the year to help you grow both personally and professionally.

Can I join for multiple roles?

No! we recommend starting with one role to ensure you can manage your commitments effectively before taking on additional responsibilities.

How it will benefit my career?

This provides practical experience, develops soft skills, expands your professional network, and demonstrates initiative to future employers. You'll also receive certificates of participation and potentially recommendation letters for outstanding contributors.
