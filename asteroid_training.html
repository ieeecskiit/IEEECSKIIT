About the Program

This intensive two-day online training program is designed to prepare students for the International Astronomical Search Collaboration (IASC) in collaboration with NASO. Participants will learn to use Astrometrica software to identify and report potential asteroid discoveries.

Format: Online live training sessions | Duration: 2 days (4 hours total) | Certificate provided

Objective: Equip students with practical skills in asteroid detection and data analysis, contributing to real scientific research.

Prerequisites: Basic computer skills. No prior astronomy experience required.

Training Details

Day 1

Introduction & Software Setup

  • Introduction to AstroSource Campaign
  • NASO collaboration overview
  • Importance of asteroid search
  • Introduction to Astrometrica Software
  • Downloading & Installing Astrometrica
  • Downloading Practice Image Sets
  • Setting up Astrometrica
  • Loading Images into Astrometrica
Day 2

Hands-on Practice & Report Submission

  • Working with Practice Image Sets
  • Step-by-step asteroid detection workflow
  • Identifying moving objects
  • Practice Sessions in Breakout Rooms
  • Criteria for Asteroid Detection
  • Submitting Observation Reports
  • Practice report submission
  • Final Review & Q/A Session

Training Mentor

Deepti Gautam

Mentor, All-Nepal Asteroid Search Campaign (ANASC)

Butwal, Nepal

Deepti Gautam, born in 2004, is a passionate amateur astronomer and educational outreach enthusiast from the scenic region of Rupandehi. Born and raised in a culturally rich region of Nepal, Deepti's venture into the realm of astronomy was a spark that would soon become her life's pursuit.


Education played a pivotal role in shaping Deepti's astronomical aspirations. She embarked on her academic voyage at Gyan Punj Bidhya Mandir Secondary School, where she nurtured her curiosity and laid the foundation for her future endeavors. Moving forward, she pursued higher education at Horizon GBSS, a stepping stone that propelled her towards the limitless expanse of the universe.


The pursuit of knowledge led Deepti to New Horizon College, where she delved into the intricacies of the cosmos, honing her skills and gaining insights into the celestial wonders that captivated her imagination. This educational chapter not only equipped her with the necessary tools for astronomical exploration but also kindled a desire to share her passion with others.


Deepti's commitment to fostering a love for astronomy among her fellow Nepalis became evident through her involvement as a Junior Member at the Nepal Astronomical Society (NASO). Here, she not only expanded her own understanding of the universe but also actively contributed to the astronomical community in Nepal.

Undeterred by the challenges, Deepti took a giant leap in her astronomical journey by founding Astronomy Enthusiasts In Nepal-AEN, an organization that serves as a platform for like-minded individuals to share their passion for the cosmos. As the Founder and Executive Head of AEN, Deepti has played a crucial role in creating a vibrant community of astronomy enthusiasts in Nepal.


In addition to her organizational leadership, Deepti is an avid public speaker and lecturer on astronomy, sharing her knowledge and enthusiasm with audiences eager to explore the wonders of the universe. She is actively involved in showcasing the beauty of the night sky to the public through her telescope, fostering a deeper connection between people and the cosmos.

Deepti's creativity extends beyond scientific exploration; she is also known for her beautiful poetry about the universe, expressing the awe and wonder that celestial phenomena evoke in her.


In her research endeavors, Deepti has found a home at the American Association of Variable Star Observers (AAVSO), where she channels her passion into meaningful contributions to the field. Her work at AAVSO reflects a commitment to advancing scientific knowledge and contributing to the global community of variable star observers.


Beyond her academic and creative pursuits, Deepti calls the picturesque town of Butwal home. Living amidst the natural beauty of West Nepal, she finds inspiration in both the celestial wonders above and the terrestrial wonders below.

Deepti Gautam's story is one of curiosity, passion, and a relentless pursuit of understanding the cosmos. As she continues her journey, she remains a beacon of inspiration for people young and old in Nepal and beyond. Regularly participating in the Explore Alliance's Global Star Party, Deepti continues to share her passion for astronomy on a global scale.
