About the Workshop

This comprehensive workshop is designed to take you from beginner to proficient in Autodesk Fusion 360, one of the most powerful 3D CAD/CAM/CAE tools available today. Whether you're an engineering student, designer, or hobbyist, this workshop will equip you with practical skills for product design and development.

Format: Online live webinar series | Duration: 9 days | Certificate provided

Objective: Provide hands-on training in 3D modeling, simulation, and rendering using Fusion 360, with a special focus on design patent processes.

Prerequisites: Basic computer skills. No prior CAD experience required.

Workshop Mentor

Er. Arbind Kumar Yadav

Chief Technology Officer (CTO), Voyager Mayeen

Arbind K. Yadav is a Mechanical Engineer from Nepal with a distinguished career spanning data analytics, aerospace engineering, and leadership roles in pioneering space technology ventures. He began his professional journey as a Data Analyst before transitioning into aerospace engineering, where he specialized in propulsion systems.

He previously served as Lead Propulsion Engineer at Mach24 Orbitals and currently holds the position of Chief Technology Officer at Voyager Mayeen, both of which are Nepal's leading companies developing small satellite launch vehicles. In addition to his engineering expertise, he has filed and been granted multiple design patents, with several utility patents presently in progress.

With over two years of professional experience in Autodesk Fusion 360, particularly applied to rocket technology, he is passionate about sharing his knowledge through this training program. His goal is to help participants unlock new dimensions in design, empowering them to transform their imagination into tangible creations.
